Age is a special case. The minimum age for an investigator is EDU + 6 years. For each ten years older than that, we'll add one point of EDU and grant an additional 20 occupation points (more on those later). However: for each 10 years above age 40, we'll subtract one point from STR, CON, DEX, or APP (your choice, with the default being STR).

If you decide to modify your investigator's age, enter the new age, select the characteristic you'd like the penalty to be subtracted from, and click the "Set Age" button below. This will modify your investigator's age and apply the penalty (if any) and isn't undoable.

To determine your investigator's annual income and additional assets, select an Era from the dropdown and click the "(Re)Roll Income" button below.
