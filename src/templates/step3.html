Step 3: Determine Education

The third roll, for Education, is generated by rolling 3d6+3:

  • EDU: Education is the measure of the combined formal and experiential knowledge an investigator possesses. It also indicates the time taken to learn that material. EDU measures the quantity of information, not the ability to use that information intelligently. This characteristic is used to determine how many skill points an investigator begins with, the Know roll, and the starting percentage in the Own Language skill.

Only a single dice roll is available here (in the green circle). Click it, and then click the gray circle next to EDU. If you're unhappy with the roll, click the "Reroll" button below. When you're satisfied, click the "Next" button.
