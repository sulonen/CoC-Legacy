Step 2: Assign secondary characteristic scores

The second set of characteristics are created by rolling 2d6+6:

  • SIZ: Size combines height and weight and is a factor in the calculation of damage and hit points.
  • INT: Intelligence represents how well investigators learn, remember, and analyze, and of how aware they are of their surroundings. This characteristic determines the number of personal interest skill points an investigator starts with, how quickly Cthulhu Mythos spells can be learned, and is used in calculating the Idea roll.

Dice rolls are again provided in the right column (in the green circles). Click one, and then click the gray circle next to the characteristic you'd like to assign it to. If you're unhappy with the rolls, click the "Reroll" button below. When you're satisfied, click the "Next" button.
