Step 6: (Occupations and) Skills

The standard Call of Cthulhu way to handle occupations and skills involves selecting an occupation from a list, applying Occupation Points only to skills associated with that occupation, and then allocating Interest Points freely among all available skills.

This method's fine if you're just starting out or if you need to roll up an Investigator quickly. In general, we (our CoC gaming group) like to pool the points and spend them as we'd like to. That's what I've done here.

You're welcome to spend your points according to the standard method (that's why I've provided the two points categories), but you'll need to use the list in the book or work with your Keeper (GM) to create a custom occupation.

Enter percentiles in the boxes to the right of the skills on the next page. The Total Points number will decrease as you spend your points. If you go over, the number will turn red and stay that way until you fix the percentiles. Do make sure to spend all your points though; unspent points are discarded.

Please note: The Cthulhu Mythos skill can only be increased by exposure to the Mythos. It can't be purchased or upgraded like other skills.

If you want to start over, just click the "Reset" button. When you're done, click the "Next" button.
