This is a tool intended to simplify the creation of Investigators for the Call of Cthulhu (CoC) roleplaying game.

From Chaosium's website:

Call of Cthulhu is a horror roleplaying game using the Basic Roleplaying system and based upon the writings of Howard Phillips Lovecraft and a few others. Lovecraft wrote during the 1920s and 1930s, and he became a cult figure before dying in 1937. Since then his stature as an author has grown, and now he is generally recognized as the major American horror-story writer of the twentieth century.

7th Edition rules are available now (as of late 2015), but my group's using the 6th Edition rules (published in 2005) for a variety of reasons. In general, we'll be using the rules for Investigator creation directly from that edition of the rule book. I've allowed for a few small changes to the process described there (such as the assignment of characteristic rolls to better support a player's desired profession).

Keep in mind that an Investigator tends to differ from characters in many other RPG systems. They aren't (in general) intended to be damage-producing machines. How you decide to allocate your skill points is probably going to be more important than how many 18s you roll. Yes, there are Reroll buttons. Of course there are.
